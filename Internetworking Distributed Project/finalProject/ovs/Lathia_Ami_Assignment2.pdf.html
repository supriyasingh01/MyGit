Welcome to the USC Viterbi School of Engineering's Office of Graduate and Professional Programs online learning portal powered by Blackboard through the Distance Education Network (DEN). This portal allows students and professional program participants enrolled in a distance learning section to logon and gain access to course materials. Please note this portal address www.uscden.net in case you experience difficulty with the DEN main page you should try access via this portal.
 
  
All Blackboard Users
Haven't Received Your Password Yet? If you have not received your password, try using the "Forgot Password?" link found on the login page. Please use all lower-case letters for your first name, last name, and username.
Fall, Spring, Summer Semester Students
Course links for the new semester will appear 2 days before classes start for all enrolled students. Students who enroll after this time can expect the link to courses to take up to 3 days to appear. Course pages will be available for two weeks after final exams, but webcast lectures of courses will not be available after the last day of finals.
