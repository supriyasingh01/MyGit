Sid
111-20

--
Summary:
This event is generated when the stream4 preprocessor encounters a situation where the preprocessor is not inspecting traffic in order to mitigate problems within the process itself.

--
Impact:
The preprocessor may discard data. The inspection of traffic may be impaired.

--
Detailed Information:
This event is generated when the stream4 preprocessor encounters a situation where the preprocessor is not inspecting traffic in order to mitigate problems within the process itself.

The system is in a self-preservation state, most likely due to the preprocessor not being able to process all traffic.

--
Affected Systems:
All networked systems.
