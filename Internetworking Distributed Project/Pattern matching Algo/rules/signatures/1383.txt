Rule:

--
Sid:
1383

--
Summary:
This event is generated when network traffic that indicates Fastrack kazaa/morpheus is being used. This is a Peer-to-Peer (P2P) application.

--
Impact:
Possible policy violation. The use of Fastrack kazaa/morpheus may be prohibited by corporate policy in some network environments. 

--
Detailed Information:
This event indicates that the Fastrack kazaa/morpheus is being used on the protected network.

Peer-to-Peer applications may also be used as a vector to transfer worms and viruses past Anti-Virus defenses onto a protected network.

--
Affected Systems:
All systems using Fastrack kazaa/morpheus

--
Attack Scenarios:
This is a possible policy violation, it may be that Fastrack kazaa/morpheus has been installated on a client host.

Peer-to-Peer clients can be used to transmit confidential corporate information out of the protected network.

--
Ease of Attack:
Simple.

--
False Positives:
None known.

--
False Negatives:
None known.

--
Corrective Action:
Disallow the use of Fastrack kazaa/morpheus on the protected network and enforce or implement an organization wide policy on the use of Fastrack kazaa/morpheus.
